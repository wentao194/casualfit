Hi, I'm Wes

I'm a guy that lifts casually

The journey started around 7 years ago

Like most people that I know, I started lifting weights to simply look good. Over the years, I drowned myself into the world of bodybuilding and absorbed as much information as I could. I diligently applied all that I learned and gained extremely valable experiences. Half way through college, I started to help train some of my friends.

It was then that I realize that I have a passion to help others to advancd their fitness goals. Also at the same time, I realize that most people are not into fitness as I was. The reason was that for most people, hardcore training, healthy eating, and proper rest does not fit everyone's lifestyle...

most people simply want to lift casually

When the realization hit me, I shifted my training techniques. Instead of dumping all the technicality of lifting and nutrition on my trainees. I decided to focus on three main ideas. These are functional strength, aesthetics, and raw strength.
